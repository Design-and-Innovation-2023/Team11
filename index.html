    Project Title

    Team 6A: Barclay’s Impact

    Project Description

    Discover Barclay’s Innovative Auto Donate App, Barclay’s Impact, an inventive solution that revolutionizes charitable donation by aligning it with your lifestyle and preferences. Witness how we've transformed donations into a personalized, seamless, and impactful journey. Discover our commitment to creating a groundbreaking, user-centric experience that redefines philanthropy.

    Reflection

    Through collective effort, the design and innovation course deepened our grasp of user-centered design and the transformative power of innovation in problem-solving.

    Team 6A
    Ian Lim

    learnt the importance of how working through the innovative design process leads to a more effective and unique solution.

    Zamora

    I learnt the design process to help designers and teams iterate, improve, and create products that meet user needs and deliver a positive user experience.

    Cyrus

    This project enabled a deeper understanding of the design process, where I have learnt product development from conception to fruition.

    Loo Xian Hui

    The course provided invaluable insights into iterative creativity, user-centered design, and transformative innovation, shaping my problem-solving skills and mindset positively.

    Munigal

    I learnt the value of regular check-ins and diverse stakeholder feedback which served as guiding elements for our final product.

    Wei Jie

    Through collective effort, the design and innovation course deepened our grasp of user-centered design and the transformative power of innovation in problem-solving.
